How to Play

Whack-a-keyboard is a typing speed and reaction time game where you need to press the highlighted keys before they expire.

When a key lights up, press it as quickly as possible to score points. The faster you press it, the more points you'll earn!

Different difficulty levels change how quickly you need to press each key:

  • Zen: No time pressure
  • Easy: 5 seconds per key
  • Medium: 2 seconds per key
  • Difficult: 1 second per key
  • Hell: Only 0.5 seconds per key!

Choose a time limit for your game or play infinitely in the "Infinite" mode.

Press ESC at any time to end the game.
